Helos

Smart Football Cleat

Role Proof of Concept
Timeline 2021
Skills Industrial Design, Research, Prototyping

Overview

A comprehensive exploration of smart footwear design for athletes. This thesis project investigates the intersection of wearable technology and performance sports equipment, creating a football cleat that enhances player performance through intelligent design.

The Helos smart cleat integrates sensors and feedback systems to provide real-time performance data while maintaining the comfort and functionality expected from professional-grade athletic footwear.
